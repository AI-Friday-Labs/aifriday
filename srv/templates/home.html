Every Friday in New Orleans, we get together and share what we’re building with AI. Bring your laptop, bring your questions, bring a friend. That’s it.

AI Friday is a casual meetup — no panels, no keynotes, no “thought leadership.” Just people showing cool stuff they’ve been tinkering with. Wanna demo that side project? Awesome. Want to just watch and ask questions? Also awesome.

We meet on Fridays, keep it short, and usually end up at a bar after. If you’re curious about AI and happen to be in New Orleans, you’re in.
