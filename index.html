What is this

A Friday morning meetup in New Orleans

We get together once a month to show what we're building with AI, help each other get unstuck, and talk about what's new. Some people demo things. Some people ask questions. It's low-key.

Mostly startup founders and people who work at startups. You don't need to be technical. You don't need to have something to show. You just need to be curious enough to give up a Friday morning.
