⚠️ The Cognitive Cost of AI

"Cognitive Surrender": Research Finds AI Users Abandon Critical Thinking

Experiments show large majorities accepting "faulty" AI answers without question. When an LLM confidently gives you a wrong answer, most people just... trust it. This is a real cognitive risk: we're outsourcing judgment to systems that hallucinate. Not doom, but it's the kind of research that should make you pause before handing everything to an agent.

The Cognitive Impact of Coding Agents
