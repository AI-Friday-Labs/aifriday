AI Friday is a monthly morning meetup in New Orleans for founders and operators who are building with AI — or ready to start. No pitches, no panels. Just people helping each other keep up.

Show & tell, not show & impress.

People share what they’re working on — half-built prototypes, weekend hacks, workflows they just figured out. Nothing needs to be finished. Nothing needs to be a business.

Get unstuck.

Bring your laptop, bring your questions. Someone in the room has hit the same wall and found a way through.

Stay connected.

Between meetups, the Slack keeps going — sharing tools, asking questions, helping each other move faster.

Things are moving so fast that even experienced builders feel like they’re falling behind. We started AI Friday because we felt it too.

This isn’t a networking event. It’s not a happy hour. It’s a small group of people who show up on a Friday morning because they want to learn, help, and keep moving.

If you’re building a startup in New Orleans and AI is part of your future — whether you’re already shipping or just getting started — you’re who this is for.
