Meeting #1 — Kickoff

Friday, June 13, 2025 · 9:00–11:00 AM · Mojo Coffee, Magazine St

First meeting. Twelve people showed up, which was more than expected. Good mix of founders and ops people. A few devs. Went about 90 minutes.

Who was there

Andrew D. Eli A. Sarah M. James K. Nina P. Chris L. Devon R. Priya S. Marcus T. Leigh B. Oscar W. Ava C.

Demos & Discussion

Andrew showed the AI Friday daily brief bot. It pulls from RSS feeds and newsletters, scores items, and posts a curated summary to Slack every morning. Built with Go and Claude.

Eli walked through how he's using Claude Code to scaffold entire features from a single prompt. The group got into a good discussion about when to trust AI-generated code vs. when to rewrite.

Sarah demoed a Perplexity workflow for competitive research. She chains 3-4 queries and exports to Notion. Took about 10 minutes, replaced what used to take her half a day.
