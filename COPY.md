# AI Friday — Copy & Positioning

## Core Truth
AI is moving so fast that smart, experienced people feel behind.
They're not behind — they just don't have the right room to walk into.
AI Friday is that room.

## Audience
Startup founders, operators, and business leads in New Orleans.
People who are technical enough to use the CLI but spend most of their day
making decisions, not writing code. Mix of "already building with AI" and
"know I should be but haven't started."

## Emotional Promise
1. You're not behind.
2. You don't have to figure this out alone.
3. You'll leave with something concrete.

---

## Headline Options

### A — "Keep up together"
Simple. The two anxieties (speed + isolation) in three words.

### B — "AI moves fast. You don't have to figure it out alone."
Says the quiet part out loud. More vulnerable, more human.

### C — "Your Friday morning edge."
Positions the meetup as a competitive advantage. More startup energy.

**Recommendation: B for the site hero.** It's honest and it's exactly
what someone Googling "AI meetup New Orleans" needs to hear.
A works great as a tagline / Slack channel topic.

### What Happens

**Show & tell, not show & impress.**
People share what they're working on — half-built prototypes,
weekend hacks, workflows they just figured out. Nothing needs
to be finished. Nothing needs to be a business.

**Get unstuck.**
Bring your laptop, bring your questions. Someone in the room
has hit the same wall and found a way through.

**Stay connected.**
Between meetups, the Slack keeps going — sharing tools,
asking questions, helping each other move faster.

### About
Things are moving so fast that even experienced builders feel like
they're falling behind. We started AI Friday because we felt it too.

This isn't a networking event. It's not a happy hour. It's a small
group of people who show up on a Friday morning because they want
to learn, help, and keep moving.

If you're building a startup in New Orleans and AI is part of your
future — whether you're already shipping or just getting started —
you're who this is for.

## What This Is NOT (internal, not on site)
- Not a developer meetup (though devs are welcome)
- Not a happy hour with an AI label
- Not a pitch event or demo day
- Not for people who want to debate whether AI is good or bad
- Not "thought leadership" — it's people in the arena helping each other

---

## Tone Guide
- Direct, warm, confident
- Acknowledge the anxiety without feeding it
- "We" not "you" — we're all in this together
- No hype, no breathlessness, no "revolutionary"
- Okay to be funny but don't try hard
- New Orleans energy: unhurried, genuine, no pretense
